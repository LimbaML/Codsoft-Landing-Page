About Palm and Pine Restaurant

Welcome to Palm and Pine, where culinary excellence meets warm hospitality. Nestled in the heart of the city, our restaurant is a haven for those seeking an exquisite dining experience with a focus on tantalizing non-vegetarian delicacies and indulgent desserts.

At Palm and Pine, we take pride in crafting a menu that caters to the discerning palate of our patrons. Our chefs curate a diverse selection of non-vegetarian dishes, ranging from succulent steaks and flavorful seafood to mouthwatering grilled specialties. Each dish is meticulously prepared using fresh, high-quality ingredients to ensure a memorable dining experience.

Whether you're a connoisseur of savory flavors or have a sweet tooth that craves perfection, Palm and Pine invites you to embark on a culinary adventure that harmonizes taste, ambiance, and exceptional service. Join us for an unforgettable dining experience that blends passion and gastronomy.

Classic Hot Dog

Savor the timeless taste of our classic hot dog, featuring a juicy, perfectly grilled sausage nestled in a soft, fresh bun. Topped with your choice of mustard, ketchup, onions, and relish, it's a go-to favorite for hot dog enthusiasts.

Chili Cheese Dog

Spice up your hot dog experience with our Chili Cheese Dog! Indulge in the delicious combination of a hearty beef sausage, savory chili, and melted cheddar cheese, all encased in a warm bun. It's a flavor explosion that will leave you craving more.

Gourmet Hot Dog

Elevate your hot dog game with our Gourmet Hot Dog options. From artisanal sausages to unique toppings like truffle mayo and caramelized onions, these hot dogs redefine the classic street food experience.

Veggie Delight Momos

Our Veggie Delight Momos are a treat for vegetarians! These steamed dumplings are filled with a flavorful mixture of finely chopped vegetables, herbs, and spices. Served with a tangy dipping sauce, they are a perfect blend of lightness and taste.

Chicken Dumplings

Indulge in the succulent goodness of our Chicken Momos. Tender minced chicken, seasoned to perfection, is encased in delicate dumpling wrappers and steamed to juicy perfection. Pair them with our signature spicy dipping sauce for an extra kick.

Prawn Momos

For seafood lovers, our Prawn Momos are a must-try! Enjoy the exquisite taste of fresh prawns wrapped in thin, translucent dumpling skins. Lightly steamed, these momos showcase the natural flavors of the sea.
